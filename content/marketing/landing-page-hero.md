## Copy Principles for Hero

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Use numbers (500K, 15+, $1.99)
- Show outcomes (jobs, games, progress)
- Emphasize access (no laptop, no cost barrier)
- Create urgency (join thousands, limited time on free features)

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Use jargon (don't say "pedagogical spiral," say "learn by doing")
- Overload (3 sentences max before CTA)
- Make promises without backing (don't say "guaranteed job," say "job-ready")

## Mobile Hero (Phone Screen)

Adjust for mobile:
- Headline: 20-24px
- Shorter subheading (2 lines max)
- CTA button: Full width, bottom-friendly
- Video smaller or removed (may autoplay muted)
- Trust signals in carousel, not grid

## A/B Testing Recommendations

**Test 1:** Problem vs. Solution
- Version A: "Learn Without a Laptop" (problem)
- Version B: "Code Everywhere" (solution)

**Test 2:** Outcome vs. Process
- Version A: "Land Your $120K Job" (outcome)
- Version B: "Learn 15+ Languages" (process)

**Test 3:** Urgency
- Version A: "Start Free Today"
- Version B: "Join 500K Students Learning Now"

Run each for 2 weeks, measure: CTR, signup rate, quality of signups.

## Conversion Optimization Tips

1. **Button:** Use contrasting color (if site is blue, use orange/yellow)
2. **Form:** No form on hero. Direct download link (reduces friction)
3. **Video:** Auto-play muted, fast-paced (keep attention)
4. **Mobile:** Stack content vertically (not side-by-side)
5. **Loading:** Fast hero load (<2s)
6. **Clarity:** No ambiguity. What happens when I click? "Download free app."
